A simple, lightweight algorithm that checks specific facebook data against a sample (rishi) to determine whether or not you are a potential bride for the man.

Likes - Up to 20% similarity
Recent Events - Up to 20% similarity
Hometown - 5% all or nothing
Education - Up to 15% similarity
Age Range - 5% all or nothing
Mutual Friends - Up to 20%
Location - Up to 5%
TBD - Last 10%

Total : 90
